❤️ Your Support Can Shape Their Future ❤️

Recently, floods have affected nearly 1,000 institutions and 60,000 impoverished students in various regions of our country. Especially for Hifz students, who are memorizing the Quran, this situation is extremely critical. Without nutritious food, proper medical care, and copies of the Quran, they may suffer mentally and physically, and there is a risk of them forgetting the parts of the Quran they have memorized.

We, CrestWave, a humanitarian aid organization, have taken the initiative to implement an emergency project for these students. Our project aims to:

  • Provide nutritious food for Hifz students.
  • Deliver copies of the Quran to the students.
  • Set up temporary classrooms to resume educational activities.
