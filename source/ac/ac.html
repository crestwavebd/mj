Split AC in Bangladesh

Split AC in Bangladesh is the most common type of air conditioner that you will find in homes that have installed an AC. Before the split AC came, people used to use window AC. A split AC has two main parts- the indoor unit and the outdoor unit. The indoor unit is located inside the room and the outdoor unit as the name suggests stays outside the room. Previous ACS needed a ductwork system for the units to connect. However, with split ACs the ductwork system isn’t needed. Rather, the two units are connected via a set of pipes. Refrigerant is dispersed through the copper pipes that cycle through the system to generate either heated or cold air. Energy-wise, a split system with the highest ENERGY STAR rating not only allows your system to be more energy-efficient, but it also helps to keep the environment clean and lower your monthly power bill by using less energy.
If it’s time for a new ac unit installation or time to replace an older system with a more energy-efficient system, here is an energy-efficient option you’ll want to consider. One of the most common air conditioning systems that work well in homes without ductwork as an addition to your current heating and cooling system is the split air conditioner system.
