Frequently Asked Questions (Hidden Answers)

What is this donation for?
This donation will support our various community projects and initiatives.
How can I donate?

Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
Can I donate anonymously?
Yes, you can choose to leave the name field blank if you wish to donate anonymously.

You will receive a receipt via email after your donation is processed.
